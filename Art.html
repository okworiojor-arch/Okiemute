WATER


What is water ?

Water is regarded as the universal solvent. It is a good solvent for many subtances.

Source of water

  1. Natural water: Rain water, Well water, Spring water and Sea water
  2. Treated water: Distilled water, Pipe-brone water and Chlorinated water.

Types of water

Water is of two types namely: Soft water and Hard water.
Soft water forms lather with soap easily, while hard water does not form lather readily with soap since it contains dissolved salt in it.

Advantage of hard water

  1. It has better taste than soft water
  2. Calcium salts in it helps to build strong teeth and bones
  3. It provides CaCO3, that crab and snail use to build their shelts
  4. It does not dissolve lesd, hence it can be supplied in lead pipes

Disadvantage of hard water

  1. It causes furing of kettles and boilers
  2. It waste soap
  3. it cannot be used in dyeing and tanning

Importance of water

  1. Water is the most precious gift of nature. It is essential for all living beings on Earth. Without water, life is not possible. Humans, animals, and plants all need water to survive.
  2. Water is used for drinking, cooking, bathing, washing, and framing. Industries also need water for ptoducing goods. Framers depend on water for growing crops. Clean water keeps us healthy and free from diseases.

Structure of water

In molecules of water,H2O,the central atom which(O) is oxygen while the (H2) is two hydrogen.

Conculsion

Water is life: we should use water wisely and protect it from pollution. If we save water today, it will save us tomorrow.
